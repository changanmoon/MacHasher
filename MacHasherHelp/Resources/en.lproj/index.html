MacHasher User Guide

MacHasher is a simple file checksum calculator.

Checksums calculation

With MacHasher, you can calculate checksums (including md5, sha1, sha256 and sha512) of files, and you can copy the according values.

If you want to choose a file, click the "Change path" button. To clear all the results, just click the "Clear" button on the toolbar.
